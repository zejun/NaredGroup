Vision & Mission

Collaborative Planning

Throughout the development process, we strives to integrate the built environment into natural environ-ment, adding to the long term sustainability design.

Why United States

Warren Buffett on CNBC: I'd Buy Up 'A Couple Hundred Thousand' Single-Family Homes If I Could

Warren Buffett says along with equities, single-family homes are a very attractive investment right now. Appearing live on CNBC's Squawk Box, Buffett tells Becky Quick he'd buy up "a couple hundred thousand" single family homes if it were practical to do so. If held for a long period of time and purchased at low rates, Buffett says houses are even better than stocks. He advises buyers to take out a 30-year mortgage and refinance if rates go down. In 2007, at the height of the real estate bubble, 73 percent of Americans owned their own home. After the crises of 2008. the real estate in US reached a low state, many people lost their home. By 2012, that num-ber sunk to 62 percent. Now the market is coming up , more and more people want to buy new properties.

It is a good time to buy a house in the US

Under an open market, the real estate market in the US is not like that in China, it has up and downs as a cyclic movement. We say right now the market is good because it is at the bottom: prices are 1/3 to 1/2 of the original value during the peak (2005-2006), but with a trend coming up, as a lot of industries in US is booming.

It is feasible to buy a house in the US

Foreign investors are able to buy freehold properties anywhere in the US. In 2011, a new immigration bill has been passed titled "Increasing Home Ownership by Priority Visitors." It would offer non-immigrant vi-sas to foreigners who buy $500,000 worth or more of residential US real estate.

It is profitable to buy a house in the US

If you buy a property in US, you have the land ownership for-ever. The legal system is safe and protects land ownership.

You can gain profit from the property you buy by renting it out. A majority of US real state market is renting. The rental yields are generally very attractive : 8-10% (Annual rental fee/property cost). Mortgage interest rates are at record low (even for foreign nations).

What We do

We manage every steps of your building process, expediting the project deliverable.

The general sequence of real estate development includes
1. Planning and Initiation – Listen to your ideas, seek possibilities, provide and refine ideas, locate specific site, and then determine preliminary development concept;
2. Feasibility – Conduct market analysis, feasibility study, engineering reports, preliminary cost esti-mate, and finalize development concept;
3. Commitment – Negotiate legal contracts, prepare necessary documents, secure public sector approvals, finalize designs, and prepare final cost estimate, plans and schedules;
4. Construction – Complete engineering design, secure building permits and approvals, coordinate construction, and ensure project within budget and schedule;
5. Operation – Oversees property management, provide property maintenance, and professional services you demand.
